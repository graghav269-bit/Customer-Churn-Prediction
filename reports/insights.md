# Customer Churn Analysis Report

## Executive Summary

Customer churn significantly impacts revenue and profitability.
This report identifies the major drivers of customer churn and provides actionable business recommendations.

---

# Key Findings

## 1. Customers Most Likely To Churn

Characteristics of high-risk customers:

- Month-to-Month Contracts
- Short Tenure (<12 Months)
- Fiber Optic Users
- High Monthly Charges
- Electronic Check Payment Method

These groups show the highest churn behavior.

---

## 2. Revenue Impact

Customer churn directly reduces recurring monthly revenue.

Observed effects:

- Reduced customer lifetime value
- Increased acquisition costs
- Lower profitability

Retaining customers is considerably cheaper than acquiring new customers.

---

## 3. Contract Type Effect

Month-to-Month customers churn the most.

Customers with:

- One-Year Contracts
- Two-Year Contracts

show significantly lower churn rates.

### Recommendation

Offer discounts for long-term contracts.

---

## 4. Payment Method Effect

Customers using:

- Electronic Check

typically have higher churn.

Customers using:

- Credit Card (Automatic)
- Bank Transfer (Automatic)

are generally more loyal.

### Recommendation

Promote automatic payment methods.

---

## 5. Tenure Effect

Customers with lower tenure are more likely to leave.

Long-term customers demonstrate:

- Higher satisfaction
- Lower churn probability

### Recommendation

Improve onboarding and early engagement.

---

## Retention Strategies

### Strategy 1

Launch customer retention campaigns for:

- New customers
- High monthly charge customers

---

### Strategy 2

Provide loyalty rewards.

Examples:

- Discounts
- Premium support
- Free upgrades

---

### Strategy 3

Introduce contract upgrade incentives.

---

### Strategy 4

Implement predictive churn alerts.

Use the machine learning model to identify at-risk customers before churn occurs.

---

# Business Impact

Expected outcomes:

- Reduced churn rate
- Increased revenue
- Improved customer satisfaction
- Better customer lifetime value
